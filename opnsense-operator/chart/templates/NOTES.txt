opnsense-operator is installed in namespace "{{ .Release.Namespace }}".

It reconciles OPNsense from homelab.lab/* annotations on LoadBalancer Services
and Gateway-API Gateways:

  homelab.lab/hostname       comma-separated DNS names (wildcard ok, e.g. "*.lab")
  homelab.lab/expose         "true" to create a WAN port-forward
  homelab.lab/external-port  external WAN port (default: the service port)
  homelab.lab/protocol       tcp|udp (default: the service port protocol)
  homelab.lab/internal-port  forwarded-to port (default: the service port)

Managed zones: {{ .Values.opnsense.managedDomains | quote }}
OPNsense URL:  {{ .Values.opnsense.url | quote }}

Verify the controller is running:

  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} rollout status deploy/{{ include "opnsense-operator.fullname" . }}

Example:

  kubectl annotate svc my-svc \
    homelab.lab/hostname=my-svc.lab homelab.lab/expose=true
